2016 Travel Destinations!

Andrew's top picks for your next vacation

Domestic

  • Bourbon Trail, KY

    • The bourbon trail has everything a traveling gent could want. Bourbon, horses, and gambling on horses!

  • New Orleans, LA

    • A melting pot of cultures and cuisines by the Bayou, New Orleans is a gem of a city.

  • Buffalo, NY

    • An underrated city with a great architectural legacy and the most out of control fans in football!

International

  • Tobago

    • A caribbean island off the beaten path with real jungles and snorkeling to explore.

  • Uruguay

    • A progressive South American nation with rural charm, colonial architecture, and tropical beaches. A little something for everyone!
